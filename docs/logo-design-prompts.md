## Option 1: Minimalist QR + Chat Logo (RECOMMENDED)

**Prompt:**

```
Design a professional, minimalist app icon logo for a restaurant technology SaaS called "Lingua".

The logo should combine:
1. A subtle QR code pattern (only the 3 corner finder squares, not a full QR)
2. A clean chat bubble overlapping or integrated with the QR pattern
3. Circular app icon shape with rounded corners

IMPORTANT - Keep it SIMPLE:
- Maximum 3-4 visual elements total
- Do NOT overcomplicate the design
- Clean geometric shapes only
- Each element should be clearly visible at small sizes
- Less is more - choose quality over quantity

Style requirements:
- Flat design, NO gradients, NO shadows, NO 3D effects
- Modern and professional, suitable for B2B SaaS
- Use teal color (#0F766E) as primary, with orange accent (#F97316) OR white elements on teal background
- Geometric, clean lines with precise alignment
- Minimalist approach - less is more
- NO decorative elements, NO sparkles, NO generic AI patterns
- Similar style to Slack, WhatsApp, or Telegram app icons
- Must work at small sizes (16x16, 32x32, 64x64)

The design should communicate:
- QR code scanning (restaurant menu technology)
- AI chat/communication (multilingual assistant)
- Professional and trustworthy (B2B product)

Output as a clean vector-style design with sharp edges and professional finish.
```

## Tips for Best Results:

### If using Midjourney:

- Add `--v 6 --style raw --no gradient,shadow,3d,decorative` to the prompt
- Use `--ar 1:1` for square app icon
- Example: `[prompt] --v 6 --style raw --ar 1:1 --no gradient,shadow,3d`

### If using DALL-E 3:

- Add: "Create this as a flat vector illustration with clean lines and solid colors only. No gradients, shadows, or 3D effects."

### If using Stable Diffusion:

- Use negative prompt: "gradient, shadow, 3d, realistic, photorealistic, decorative, ornate, complex, busy, cluttered"
- Use CFG scale: 5-7 for more creative control

### What to Avoid (AI-typical patterns):

❌ Gradient backgrounds (ANY gradients - teal, blue, purple, all bad)
❌ Glowing effects or neon
❌ 3D shadows or depth
❌ Decorative sparkles, stars, or dots
❌ Generic "tech" patterns (circuit boards, networks, nodes)
❌ Overly complex details (more than 4 elements)
❌ Generic chat bubble with three dots inside
❌ Rainbow or multi-color schemes (max 2-3 colors)
❌ Blurry or soft edges
❌ Overlapping transparency effects
❌ Complex QR codes with many small modules
❌ Decorative rings, circles, or frames around the logo

### What Makes It Professional & Simple:

✅ Flat design with solid colors ONLY
✅ Maximum 3-4 visual elements (QR squares + chat bubble = perfect)
✅ Precise geometric alignment
✅ Clean, sharp edges
✅ Scalable to 16x16 pixels and still recognizable
✅ Clear visual hierarchy (one main element, one accent)
✅ Brand-consistent colors (teal #0F766E, orange #F97316)
✅ White space / breathing room between elements
✅ Instant understanding (no need to "figure out" the logo)

### Examples of GOOD simplicity:

- WhatsApp: Just a phone in a bubble (2 elements)
- Slack: Just a hashtag shape (1 element)
- Telegram: Just a paper plane (1 element)
- QR code apps: Just 3 finder squares (3 elements)

### Examples of BAD AI-styled:

- Multiple overlapping shapes with transparency
- Gradient backgrounds with decorative blobs
- Chat bubble with dots + QR + waves + sparkles
- Complex nested elements that blur at small sizes
