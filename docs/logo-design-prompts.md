## Option A: Abstract Geometric Mark (RECOMMENDED)

Konsep: bentuk geometris yang **tidak terikat satu industri**, tapi memancarkan growth, connection, dan warmth.

Ide bentuk yang bisa dieksplor (pilih SATU):

- Sudut-sudut rounded yang membentuk monogram abstrak
- Bentuk yang bisa dibaca sebagai "A" atau inisial "ai" secara halus
- Dua elemen yang saling terhubung (melambangkan platform + tenant)
- Hexagon atau grid pattern yang simple

```
Design a professional, minimalist app icon logo for "ainything" — a B2B SaaS platform
that helps Indonesian small businesses (restaurants, retail shops, service businesses)
go digital with product catalogs, QR access, and order management.

The logo must work as a PLATFORM BRAND that can extend to sub-services:
- AinyPOS (point of sale)
- AinyChat (AI assistant)
- Future sub-services

Design concept:
- ONE abstract geometric shape — clean, modern, memorable
- The shape should suggest: connection, growth, or digital commerce
- Do NOT make it look like any specific industry (not restaurant-specific, not tech-clichéd)
- Think: how Stripe, Linear, or Vercel logos work — abstract but purposeful

IMPORTANT — Keep it SIMPLE:
- Maximum 1-2 visual elements
- Single geometric form with clear personality
- Must be instantly recognizable at 16x16px
- Must look professional at 512x512px
- Should work with "ainything", "AinyPOS", "AinyChat" wordmarks beside it

Style requirements:
- Flat design, NO gradients, NO shadows, NO 3D effects
- Modern and professional, suitable for B2B SaaS
- Primary color: emerald #059669 (main brand, growth-oriented)
- Accent color: amber #F59E0B (optional, for subtle highlight only)
- White #FFFFFF for internal negative space
- Background: solid emerald #059669 OR transparent
- Clean geometric shapes with precise alignment
- NO decorative elements, NO sparkles, NO circuit boards, NO chat bubbles
- NO food/restaurant iconography, NO service-specific symbols
- Style reference: Stripe, Linear, Vercel, Notion app icons

The logo personality should communicate:
- Growth and opportunity (for UMKM owners)
- Friendly and approachable (not intimidating for small business owners)
- Professional and trustworthy (B2B product)
- Modern and digital-forward

IMPORTANT FOR GEMINI GENERATION:
- Create as FLAT vector illustration, solid colors only
- Use EXACTLY: emerald #059669, amber #F59E0B (optional), white #FFFFFF
- NO gradients, NO shadows, NO 3D, NO transparency
- Sharp, clean edges — no blur
- Geometric precision
- Should work at 16x16 pixels and still be recognizable
- Square canvas with rounded corners (like iOS app icon shape)

Output: clean vector-style design, 1:1 aspect ratio, professional finish.
```

## Option B: Lettermark "a" or "ai"

Konsep: stylized lowercase "a" atau ligatur "ai" yang modern — simple, ownable, scalable.

```
Design a professional lettermark logo for "ainything" — a B2B SaaS platform for
Indonesian small businesses (restaurants, retail, service businesses).

The logo must scale to sub-services: AinyPOS, AinyChat, and others.

Design concept:
- Stylized lowercase "a" OR ligature "ai" as the core mark
- Clean, modern sans-serif geometry — not hand-lettered, not decorative
- The letterform itself IS the logo — no additional icons needed
- Think: how "f" works for Facebook, "g" for Google, "n" for Notion

IMPORTANT — Keep it SIMPLE:
- ONE letterform, clean and geometric
- Maximum 2 colors
- Recognizable at 16x16px

Style requirements:
- Flat design, NO gradients, NO shadows, NO 3D effects
- Modern geometric sans-serif construction
- Primary: emerald #059669 on white, OR white on emerald #059669
- Optional: amber #F59E0B as a single accent stroke or dot
- Precise proportions, optical balance
- Similar quality to: Airbnb "a", Atlassian "A", Notion "N"

IMPORTANT FOR GEMINI GENERATION:
- Flat vector, solid colors only
- Colors: emerald #059669, white #FFFFFF, optional amber #F59E0B
- NO gradients, NO shadows, NO 3D
- Sharp edges, geometric precision
- Square canvas with rounded corners

Output: clean lettermark, 1:1 aspect ratio.
```

## Option C: Icon + Wordmark System

Gunakan ini jika ingin full logo system — icon di kiri, wordmark di kanan.

```
Design a complete logo system for "ainything" — a B2B SaaS platform helping Indonesian
small businesses (restaurants, retail, services) go digital.

The system must work for sub-brands: AinyPOS, AinyChat, etc. (same icon, different name).

ICON specifications (square, 1:1):
- One clean abstract geometric mark
- Suggests: growth, connection, or digital commerce
- NOT industry-specific — works for restaurants, shops, AND service businesses
- Flat, solid colors: emerald #059669 primary, white #FFFFFF for negative space
- NO gradients, NO shadows, NO 3D
- Readable at 16x16px

WORDMARK specifications:
- "ainything" in clean, modern geometric sans-serif
- Weight: medium or semibold — not too light, not too heavy
- Color: dark #1A1A2E on light backgrounds
- The "ai" prefix should feel deliberate — this is an AI-enabled platform
- Icon aligned to left, wordmark to right, vertically centered

SUB-SERVICE ADAPTATION example (show this too):
- Same icon + "AinyPOS" wordmark
- Same icon + "AinyChat" wordmark
- Demonstrate the system works for sub-brands

Overall quality target:
- Stripe, Vercel, Linear level of design quality
- Minimalist, no decorative elements
- Professional enough for B2B enterprise
- Approachable enough for small business owners

IMPORTANT FOR GEMINI:
- Flat vector, solid colors only
- Colors: #059669 (emerald), #F59E0B (amber accent, optional), #1A1A2E (text), #FFFFFF (white)
- NO gradients, shadows, 3D, decorative elements
- Sharp geometric edges
- Provide: icon only + full lockup with wordmark

Output: clean vector-style, professional finish.
```

## Tips Gemini Pro — Optimized

### Yang Bekerja Baik di Gemini Pro

```
CRITICAL INSTRUCTIONS:
- Flat vector illustration ONLY — solid fill, no gradients
- Colors: EXACTLY #059669 (emerald), #F59E0B (amber, optional), #FFFFFF (white)
- NO: gradients, shadows, glow, 3D, transparency, blur
- Sharp geometric edges — precision over softness
- Minimal: 1-2 elements maximum
- Square 1:1 canvas
- Must be legible at 16x16 pixels
```

### Parameter yang Direkomendasikan

- Aspect ratio: **1:1** (square)
- Style: Flat vector illustration, corporate logo
- Quality: High (1024x1024px minimum)

### Kelemahan Gemini yang Perlu Diwatch

- Cenderung tambah gradient tipis — tegaskan "NO gradients" berkali-kali
- Cenderung tambah drop shadow halus — tambahkan "NO shadows of any kind"
- Cenderung over-detail — minta "MAXIMUM 2 elements, less is more"
- Kadang pakai warna di luar palette — minta "use EXACTLY these hex codes"

### Iterasi yang Efektif

1. Generate 4-6 variasi dengan prompt utama
2. Pilih yang paling clean dan simple
3. Minta adjustment spesifik: "remove the gradient", "make the shape bolder", "remove inner details"
4. Jangan minta terlalu banyak perubahan dalam satu prompt

## Anti-Patterns — Jangan Generate Ini

```
AVOID these patterns:
- QR code patterns (too niche, dates quickly)
- Chat bubbles (over-used in AI apps)
- Generic "A" letterform without personality
- Food/restaurant iconography (restaurant-only)
- Circuit boards, nodes, networks (generic tech)
- Gradient backgrounds (any gradient)
- Glowing/neon effects
- 3D depth or shadows
- Decorative rings or frames around the icon
- Multiple overlapping shapes with transparency
- Sparkles, stars, dots as decoration
- Rainbow or multi-color schemes (max 3 colors)
- Complex QR codes
- Human figures or hand illustrations
- Globe or map elements (too generic)
```

## Sub-Service Branding System

Setelah logo utama selesai, sub-service menggunakan sistem yang sama:

```
[Icon ainything] + [Nama sub-service wordmark]
```

Contoh:

- `[icon] ainything` — platform utama
- `[icon] AinyPOS` — point of sale
- `[icon] AinyChat` — AI chat assistant
- `[icon] AinyMenu` — digital menu (restoran)

Icon selalu SAMA. Hanya wordmark yang berubah.

Untuk generate variasi sub-service, gunakan prompt:

```
Using the exact same icon mark from the ainything logo, create a lockup with the
wordmark "[Sub-service name]" in the same geometric sans-serif style.
Same colors, same proportions, just different text.
```
